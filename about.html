Who We Are

Professional transport company delivering excellence across Europe

VIVS Quality s.r.o. is a reliable logistics and transport company based in Bratislava, Slovakia. We specialize in international delivery, courier services and cargo transport using vans up to 3.5t, featuring modern Iveco and Fiat models in our fleet.

With experienced drivers and a commitment to excellence, we cover the entire European Union safely and efficiently. Whether you're a business looking for reliable logistics solutions or an individual needing courier services, we offer fast, punctual and professional solutions tailored to your specific needs.

Our mission is to provide seamless transport experiences that exceed expectations, building lasting relationships with our clients through reliability, transparency, and superior service quality.
